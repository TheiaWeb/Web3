    Privacy Policy

    1. PERSONAL DATA

    The personal data collected on the site results from the voluntary communication of your information (surname, first name, address, telephone number, e-mail address, etc.) when you send a message via the dedicated form, apply for a job or submit a comment on the site. This data is solely intended for internal use, to process your request and to collect site usage statistics (see article 5). This data will be kept for a maximum of 3 years, unless otherwise required by law.

    Users of the 3spacex.com website, whether professionals or private individuals, are prohibited from publishing personal data outside the fields specifically provided for this purpose. In this context, Third Space cannot be held responsible for the disclosure to third parties of personal information entered outside these fields.

    Under no circumstances may personal data collected via this site be transferred or sold to third parties.

    Pursuant to the French Data Protection Act of January 6, 1978, amended by the Act of August 9, 2004, and the General Data Protection Regulation, which came into force on May 25, 2018, you have the right to access, rectify, modify and delete data concerning you (see Article 5).

    2. PURPOSE OF COLLECTING YOUR PERSONAL DATA

    Personal data collected through this site will be processed for one or more of the following purposes:

    - The commercial processing of your request (ordering, drawing up a quotation, requesting further information, etc.);
    - Processing your application for a position or your unsolicited application;
    - Provision of content;
    - Processing requests from visitors;
    - Statistical evaluation of site traffic;
    - Management of communication operations for which you have given your prior consent;

    In the case of our customers, the processing and storage of their personal data is required by law or contractual regulations. For other purposes, the processing and storage of data is subject to the consent of site visitors and contributors.

    3. COMMUNICATION OF YOUR PERSONAL DATA

    In order to carry out any commercial, communication, information or recruitment action, or to respond to a question or request formulated via the site, the personal information of a visitor or contributor may be transmitted to the following persons or service providers:

    - Members of the Third Space who, by virtue of their duties, are authorized to manage this processing for commercial, recruitment, communication or visitor information purposes in the context of a particular request;

    - Service providers responsible for maintaining and managing the website or carrying out personal data processing operations on behalf of Third Space, who are also subject to confidentiality and security obligations.

    As specified in Article 1, under no circumstances may personal data collected via this site be transferred or sold to third parties.

    4. STORAGE AND PROTECTION OF YOUR PERSONAL DATA

    Your personal data collected via this site will be kept for a maximum period of 3 years unless legal obligations extend this period.

    The information collected via the contact forms on this site is recorded in a secure computer file by Third Space solely for the purpose of responding to your request. You may exercise your right to access, delete or rectify your personal data using the form linked to in article 5.

    We do our utmost to protect your personal data and prevent it from being disseminated. Nevertheless, in the event of a hacking attack leading to a data leak, the site manager undertakes to notify any victim of a personal data breach as soon as possible. At the same time, a documented notification of this breach will also be sent to the CNIL within a maximum of 72 hours of its discovery.

    5. REQUEST FOR ACCESS, RECTIFICATION OR DELETION OF YOUR PERSONAL DATA

    If you have already left a message via our contact form, you have provided us with personal data (first name, surname, address, e-mail address, etc.). You can ask to receive a file containing all the personal data we hold about you, including that which you have supplied to us. You can also request the deletion of your personal data via this page.

    6. COOKIE POLICY

    To facilitate navigation, avoid recurrent identification and offer optimal conditions of use, this site may install authentication cookies on your computer, which record information relating to your connection data on your terminal. These cookies expire after 30 days. You can prevent these cookies from being saved by your browser as soon as you arrive on the site by clicking on the "I refuse" button or by selecting the unwanted applications, or at any time during your visit to the site by clicking on the "confidentiality" button at the bottom left of all site pages and deselecting the cookies to which you wish to object.

    7. USE OF YOUR DATA BY GOOGLE ANALYTICS

    This website uses Google Analytics, a web analysis service provided by Google Inc ("Google"). Google Analytics uses "cookies", which are text files placed on your computer, to help the website analyze how users use the site. The information generated by the cookie about your use of the website (including your IP address) will be transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States .

    Google will use this information for the purpose of evaluating your use of the website, compiling reports on website activity for website operators and providing other services relating to website activity and internet usage. Google may also transfer this information to third parties where required to do so by law, or where such third parties process the information on Google's behalf, including in particular the publisher of this website. Google will not associate your IP address with any other data held by Google.

    You may refuse the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings on your browser, however please note that if you do this you may not be able to use the full functionality of this website. However, such deactivation may prevent you from using certain functions of this site. You may refuse the use of cookies by following the procedure indicated in article 6.

    8. UPDATING OF THE PERSONAL DATA MANAGEMENT POLICY

    Third Space reserves the right to update the present data management and use policy at any time, in order to comply with legislative requirements. All users are therefore invited to visit this page each time they consult the site in order to familiarize themselves with it.
