    Privacy Policy

    1. Introduction

    Welcome to ThirdSpace. This policy outlines our commitment to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. It explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your personal information.

    2. Data Protection Officer

    For any questions regarding this policy or your data, please contact:
    Name: CHARLES VINCENT
    Email: 3space.contact@gmail.com

    3. Data Collection and Use

    Types of Data Collected: We collect personal information such as email address via forms on our website.
    Purpose: The data is collected for various purposes, including commercial requests processing, content provision, visitor requests, site traffic evaluation, and communication operations, based on your consent.
    Legal Basis: Your consent and, for certain processing, legal or contractual requirements.
    Communication of Data: Data may be shared with members of ThirdSpace and service providers for processing as outlined, under strict confidentiality and security obligations.

    4. Data Storage and Security

    Storage Platform: Firebase, Firestore, and Firebase Cloud Functions and other secure systems.
    Retention Period: Personal data is stored for a maximum period of 1 years unless legal obligations dictate otherwise. After 1 Year of non positive actions from the clients side, all datas saved are suppresed.
    Security Measures: We implement robust security measures to protect your data, including in case of data breaches, where we will notify affected individuals and the CNIL promptly.

    5. Your Rights

    Access, Rectification, and Deletion: You can access, correct, or request deletion of your data.
    Restriction and Portability: Request restriction of processing and obtain a copy of your data in a portable format.
    Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw consent at any time.
    Objection and Complaints: Object to processing and lodge complaints with CNIL.

    6. Consent and Withdrawal

    Your provision of personal data constitutes your consent to its collection and use as described. You can withdraw consent at any time by contacting us.

    7. Cookie Policy

    Usage: Cookies are used for authentication, navigation facilitation, and optimal usage conditions.
    Management: You have control over cookie settings and can opt out as per the guidelines provided on our site.

    8. Use of Google Analytics

    We use Google Analytics for website traffic analysis. Information including your IP address may be collected by Google, in compliance with their data processing policies.

    9. Updates to the Policy

    We reserve the right to update this policy to comply with legislative requirements. Users are advised to review this page regularly.

    10. Further Information

    ThirdSpace Company is still in the process of incorporation, for more information or queries regarding this policy, please contact us directly at 3space.contact@gmail.com.
